# Business Requirements

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses such as telecommunications companies. Retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project aims to analyze customer behavior, identify the key factors contributing to churn, and build a machine learning model capable of predicting customers who are likely to leave.

---

## Business Problem

The telecom company is experiencing a significant loss of customers due to churn. Without understanding the reasons behind customer attrition, the company risks losing revenue, reducing customer lifetime value, and increasing customer acquisition costs.

The objective is to identify high-risk customers and provide actionable insights that support customer retention strategies.

---

## Business Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and service usage.
- Identify the major factors contributing to customer churn.
- Predict customers likely to churn using machine learning.
- Visualize churn trends through an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Provide business recommendations to reduce customer churn.

---

## Project Goals

- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Conduct Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Engineer meaningful features for machine learning.
- Train and compare multiple classification models.
- Evaluate model performance using standard classification metrics.
- Build an executive dashboard for business decision-making.

---

## Stakeholders

- Business Executives
- Customer Retention Team
- Marketing Team
- Customer Service Team
- Business Analysts
- Data Analysts

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

- Accurately predicts customer churn.
- Identifies the major churn drivers.
- Provides actionable business recommendations.
- Enables stakeholders to monitor churn using an interactive dashboard.
- Supports data-driven customer retention strategies.

---

## Expected Business Outcome

By identifying customers at high risk of churn and understanding the factors influencing customer attrition, the company can implement targeted retention campaigns, improve customer satisfaction, reduce revenue loss, and increase long-term customer lifetime value.
