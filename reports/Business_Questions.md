# Business Questions

This project aims to answer key business questions related to customer churn in a telecommunications company. These questions guide the data analysis, machine learning model development, and business recommendations.

---

## 1. How many customers are present in the dataset?

Understanding the size of the customer base helps measure the overall business scale.

---

## 2. How many customers have churned?

Identifying the number of churned customers provides an overview of customer attrition.

---

## 3. What is the overall churn rate?

Calculating the churn rate helps quantify the percentage of customers leaving the company.

---

## 4. Which contract type experiences the highest churn?

Analyzing churn across different contract types helps identify high-risk customer segments.

---

## 5. Which Internet Service has the highest churn?

Understanding the relationship between internet service type and churn supports service improvement strategies.

---

## 6. Which Payment Method is most associated with churn?

Analyzing payment methods helps identify behavioral patterns linked to customer attrition.

---

## 7. Which customer tenure group has the highest churn?

Customer tenure provides valuable insights into customer loyalty and retention.

---

## 8. Which cities have the highest number of churned customers?

Geographical analysis helps identify regions requiring focused customer retention efforts.

---

## 9. What are the major reasons customers leave the company?

Understanding churn reasons enables the business to address customer pain points proactively.

---

## 10. Which customer characteristics contribute most to churn?

Machine Learning feature importance helps identify the strongest predictors of customer churn.

---

## 11. Can Machine Learning accurately predict customer churn?

Building predictive models enables the company to identify high-risk customers before they leave.

---

## 12. What business strategies can reduce customer churn?

Based on analytical insights and machine learning predictions, actionable recommendations can be developed to improve customer retention.
