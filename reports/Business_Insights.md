# Business Insights

## Overview

The analysis of customer demographics, service usage, billing patterns, and churn behavior revealed several factors that significantly influence customer churn. These insights can help businesses develop targeted customer retention strategies.

---

## Key Business Insights

### 1. Month-to-Month Contract Customers Churn the Most

Customers with Month-to-Month contracts have a significantly higher churn rate compared to customers with One-Year and Two-Year contracts.

**Recommendation:**
- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts through loyalty discounts and promotional offers.

---

### 2. Fiber Optic Customers Exhibit Higher Churn

Customers subscribed to Fiber Optic Internet Services experience higher churn compared to DSL users.

**Recommendation:**
- Improve Fiber Optic service quality and customer support.
- Investigate pricing and service-related complaints.

---

### 3. Electronic Check Customers Are More Likely to Churn

Customers using Electronic Check as their payment method show a higher tendency to leave the company.

**Recommendation:**
- Promote automatic payment methods such as credit cards or bank transfers through incentives.

---

### 4. Customers With Short Tenure Are at Higher Risk

Most churn occurs during the first year of customer subscription.

**Recommendation:**
- Strengthen onboarding programs.
- Offer welcome benefits and early engagement campaigns.

---

### 5. High Monthly Charges Increase Churn Risk

Customers paying higher monthly charges tend to churn more frequently.

**Recommendation:**
- Introduce personalized pricing plans.
- Provide bundled service discounts for high-paying customers.

---

### 6. Customer Service Significantly Impacts Retention

One of the most common churn reasons is dissatisfaction with customer support.

**Recommendation:**
- Improve customer support response time.
- Train support representatives to resolve customer issues effectively.

---

### 7. Geographic Differences Exist in Customer Churn

Certain cities have a higher number of churned customers than others.

**Recommendation:**
- Launch region-specific retention campaigns.
- Investigate local market conditions affecting customer satisfaction.

---

### 8. Machine Learning Successfully Identifies High-Risk Customers

The Logistic Regression model effectively predicts customers likely to churn, enabling proactive retention efforts.

**Recommendation:**
- Integrate the predictive model into customer relationship management (CRM) systems to identify high-risk customers in advance.

---

## Overall Business Recommendation

By combining SQL analysis, Python-based exploratory data analysis, machine learning predictions, and interactive Power BI dashboards, organizations can proactively identify customers at risk of churn and implement targeted retention strategies that improve customer satisfaction, reduce revenue loss, and increase long-term customer lifetime value.
